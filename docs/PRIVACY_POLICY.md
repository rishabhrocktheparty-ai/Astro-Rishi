# Privacy Policy — Jyotish AI

**Last Updated:** March 14, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Jyotish AI ("the App") is a Vedic astrology intelligence application. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Account Information
- Email address and display name provided during registration.

### 2.2 Birth Data
- Name, date of birth, time of birth, and place of birth that you voluntarily enter for kundali (birth chart) generation.

### 2.3 Usage Data
- Questions submitted to the AI astrology chat.
- App interaction logs (screens visited, features used).

### 2.4 Device Information
- Device type, operating system version, and app version for crash reporting and compatibility.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

- **Kundali Generation:** Birth data is used exclusively to compute your astrological chart using astronomical calculations.
- **AI Interpretations:** Your chart data and questions are sent to our AI engine to generate personalized astrological interpretations.
- **Service Improvement:** Anonymized and aggregated usage data may be used to improve the accuracy of interpretations.

## 4. Data Storage & Security

- All data is stored on Supabase (backed by PostgreSQL) with encryption at rest and in transit.
- Row Level Security ensures users can only access their own data.
- Passwords are managed through Supabase Auth and are never stored in plaintext.
- We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

## 5. Third-Party Services

- **Supabase:** Database and authentication hosting.
- **DeepSeek API:** AI inference for astrological interpretations. Your chart data (not personal identifiers) may be transmitted for processing.
- **OpenAI API:** Used for generating text embeddings of book knowledge. No personal user data is sent.

## 6. Data Retention

- Your data is retained for as long as your account is active.
- You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.
- Upon account deletion, all birth data, kundalis, and conversation history will be permanently removed within 30 days.

## 7. Children's Privacy

Jyotish AI is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## 8. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access all personal data we hold about you.
- Correct inaccurate data.
- Delete your account and all data.
- Export your data.
- Withdraw consent for data processing.

## 9. Disclaimer

Jyotish AI provides astrological interpretations based on classical Vedic texts for educational and entertainment purposes only. Interpretations should not be considered as professional advice for medical, financial, legal, or any other decisions.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes via the App or email.
